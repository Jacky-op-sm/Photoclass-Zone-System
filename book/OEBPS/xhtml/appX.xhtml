Appendix X
A Brief Directory of Online Digital and Photography-Related Resources

The Internet has become a vast and prime source of free information about digital and film photography, and also a venue for the display of creative photo-related artwork. This appendix makes no attempt to provide a comprehensive listing of available resources. The following are some of my personal bookmarks that I’ve found useful or interesting. Also remember that the Internet is a volatile place, so some of the sites listed may have moved or been discontinued by the time you read this.
